## OBJECTIVE

A small business needed an online presence that actually converts:
landing page, lead capture, and follow-up that runs itself.

## EXECUTION

- Static-first landing page in Astro — fast loads, strong Lighthouse scores.
- Lead form posts straight into Airtable as a lightweight CRM.
- Automated follow-up triggers on new leads so no inquiry goes cold.
- Deployed on Vercel with zero-maintenance hosting.

## FIELD NOTES

Deliberately boring stack: the client can manage leads in Airtable
without touching code, and hosting costs nothing.

## OUTCOME

Live in production. Leads captured, followed up, and tracked automatically.
